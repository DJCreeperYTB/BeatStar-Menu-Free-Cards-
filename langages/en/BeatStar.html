Earth Day üåç

April 22nd is Earth Day. The purpose of this day is to raise awareness about climate change and support environmental protection. In honor of this, BeatStar offers you a new avatar for free.
How to get it?

  1. Open BeatStar.
  2. Go to the Backstage tab (bottom right), then to News.
  3. Click on the Earth Day news.
  4. Click on Get Reward. You will be redirected to the website https://www.earthday.org/plastic-pollution-calculator-2/ to fill out a questionnaire. It's not mandatory, but if you want to participate in the Earth Day movement, you can fill it out. If you exit the application, BeatStar believes you have completed the questionnaire and will automatically give you the reward.
  5. Return to the game, then to the main menu. You will receive your avatar box containing... A plant avatar! üå±
